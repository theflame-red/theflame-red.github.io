About Us:

We are a collective of leftists that are aiming to help educate like minded people and show the general public that there is a society possible that is true of opression which is currently demonstrated across the statist world. We have especially seen recently in light of the student pro-Palestine protests the reactionary and oppressive forces at work to stifle any dissent, and we think now is the perfect time to shine a light onto this opression for all to see. We do not see your particular ideology as relevant, so long as it anti-state, anti-capital and pro-worker (essentially, as long as you aren't an ancap, you will probably be more than welcome to join us); thanks to this, we are made up of people of a wide variety of leftist ideologies.
